About DiCicco

At DiCicco's we've been celebrating tradition since 1956! Mama and Papa DiCicco opened their first restaurant almost seventy years ago! They built their success through hard work, family values, and a deep passion for homestyle Italian cooking. Today we honor their legacy by bringing you the flavor of homestyle Italian cooking in a traditionally styled ristorante.Over the years, members the DiCicco family has opened 19 restaurants across California's Central Valley.
